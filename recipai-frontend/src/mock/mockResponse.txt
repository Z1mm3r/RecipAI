Buffalo Chicken Dip

Ingredients:

-2 cooked, shredded chicken breasts
-1 cup of crumbled blue cheese
-1/2 cup of buffalo wing sauce
-1/4 cup of mayonnaise
-1/4 cup of sour cream
-1/4 cup of chopped celery
-1/4 cup of chopped green onion
-1/4 teaspoon of garlic powder
-Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.

2. In a large bowl, mix together the chicken, blue cheese, buffalo wing sauce, mayonnaise, sour cream, celery, green onion and garlic powder.

3. Season with salt and pepper, to taste.

4. Transfer mixture to an oven-safe baking dish.

5. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until bubbly and golden.

6. Let stand for 5 minutes before serving with crackers or vegetables. Enjoy!
